Private Digital Currency

Untraceable, secure, and fungible cryptocurrency using the RingCT protocol.

Secure

Shang security comes from its decentralization, which distributed through a global peer-to-peer consensus network. Third-parties do not need to be trusted to keep your SHG safe.

Private

Shang's blockchain protects privacy in three ways. Ring signatures enable the sender to hide among other transaction outputs, stealth addresses hide the receiving address of the transaction[, and RingCT hides the amount of the transaction.

Untraceable

Send and receive payments anonymously and hide your identity. By default, each Shang transaction obfuscates the sending and receiving addresses as well as the transaction amounts.
